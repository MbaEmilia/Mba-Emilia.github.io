About Me

I design with purpose,
not just pixels.

I'm Emilia — a UI/UX and Product Designer based in Nigeria with a passion for building digital products that are both beautiful and genuinely useful. I believe good design is invisible: it guides users naturally, reduces friction, and creates moments of delight.

My work spans web platforms, admin systems, and mobile apps. I specialize in translating complex product requirements into clean, human-centered interfaces using Figma — from initial wireframes through to interactive prototypes.
